US-001 High Priority Epic 1 Regional Market Growth Dashboard

As a VP of Strategy, I want to view food delivery market growth trends by region on an interactive dashboard so that I can identify high-opportunity expansion zones and allocate resources effectively.

‚úÖ Acceptance Criteria:
  • Dashboard displays YoY growth % and CAGR (3yr & 5yr) by city/region
  • Drill-down filters available: city, cuisine type, customer segment, date range
  • Market share pie chart updates dynamically with filter selections
  • Data refreshes monthly; stale data alert shown if > 35 days old
  • Export to PDF and Excel available for reporting
US-002 High Priority Epic 1 SQL Market Sizing Queries

As a Data Analyst, I want parameterized SQL queries that extract and aggregate order data by region and time period so that I can perform rapid market sizing analysis without writing queries from scratch.

‚úÖ Acceptance Criteria:
  • Query returns total orders, revenue, AOV, and unique customers
  • Parameters: date range, region, cuisine type, order status
  • Executes in under 30 seconds on 10M+ row dataset
  • Results match Power BI dashboard figures (¬±0.1% tolerance)
US-003 High Priority Epic 2 RFM Customer Segmentation

As a Marketing Manager, I want customers segmented using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis so that I can target high-value customers with personalized campaigns and re-engage churned users.

‚úÖ Acceptance Criteria:
  • Python script generates RFM scores for all active customers
  • Minimum 5 segments: Champions, Loyal, At Risk, Recent, Lost
  • Output includes customer_id, segment label, RFM scores, and CLV estimate
  • Results exportable to Excel/CSV for CRM campaign import
  • Visualized in Power BI as interactive bar chart and scatter plot
US-004 High Priority Epic 2 Executive KPI Summary Dashboard

As a CEO, I want a single-page executive summary dashboard so that I can monitor market performance at a glance during monthly leadership reviews without needing to drill into raw data.

‚úÖ Acceptance Criteria:
  • KPI cards: Revenue, Orders, Market Share %, Customer NPS, Avg Delivery Time
  • MoM and YoY trend sparklines on each KPI card
  • RAG (Red/Amber/Green) status indicators vs. targets
  • Mobile-responsive layout (tested on iOS Safari & Android Chrome)
  • Dashboard loads in < 5 seconds on standard broadband connection
US-005 Medium Priority Epic 3 Competitor Price Tracking

As a Strategy Analyst, I want automated competitor pricing data loaded into our database weekly so that I can track our price competitiveness and trigger pricing reviews when gaps exceed thresholds.

‚úÖ Acceptance Criteria:
  • Competitor price index calculated and stored every Monday
  • Alert triggered automatically if our price gap exceeds 10% vs. top competitor
  • Historical data retained for 24-month trend analysis
  • Data source, confidence level, and timestamp documented per record
US-006 Medium Priority Epic 4 Revenue Forecasting Model

As a Finance Director, I want a Python-based revenue forecasting model with 24-month projections so that I can build accurate financial plans and scenario budgets for board presentations.

‚úÖ Acceptance Criteria:
  • Model achieves R¬≤ ‚â• 0.85 and MAE within 5% of mean monthly revenue
  • Forecasts generated for Base, Optimistic (+15%), and Pessimistic (‚àí15%) scenarios
  • Output in Excel format with monthly and annual summaries
  • Model documentation includes assumptions and limitations section
  • Re-runs automatically when new monthly actuals are loaded
